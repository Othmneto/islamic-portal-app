Mobile Calendar Sync

Step 1: Choose Your Device

Step 2: iOS Setup

  1. Open Settings > Calendar > Accounts
  2. Add Account > Other > CalDAV
  3. Enter server: calendar.yoursite.com
  4. Enter your credentials

Scan QR code for quick setup

Step 2: Android Setup

  1. Open Google Calendar app
  2. Go to Settings > Add account
  3. Select "Other" and enter calendar URL
  4. Enter your credentials
